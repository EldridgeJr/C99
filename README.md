# Court99

Court99 is a LegalTech SaaS platform built to help law firms prepare clients for court through structured, non-legal guidance. The platform focuses on client readiness before hearings by improving clarity, reducing uncertainty, and helping clients better understand what to expect throughout the court preparation process.

## What is Court99?

Court99 is designed as a client readiness platform for law firms. It supports the non-legal side of court preparation, such as mental preparation, expectation management, procedural understanding, progress tracking, and access to structured educational resources.

Court99 does **not** replace legal advice. Instead, it helps law firms deliver a better client experience by preparing clients in a more scalable, consistent, and accessible way.

## Core Idea

Many clients enter court proceedings feeling overwhelmed, underprepared, and unsure about the process ahead. Court99 helps solve this by giving law firms a digital platform where they can provide structured preparation content and readiness support before court dates.

This can include guided learning content, curated resources, progress-based preparation journeys, podcasts and media content, mock hearing preparation, Q&A style support formats, and community and engagement features.

## Key Features

- **Guided Case Writing** — Step-by-step workflows that walk clients through building their legal case
- **Course Catalog** — Structured legal education with modules, lessons, and video content
- **Document Management** — Upload, store, and share case-related documents via Supabase Storage
- **Mock Hearings** — Practice sessions to help clients prepare for court
- **Podcasts** — Legal audio content with episode streaming
- **Q&A Sessions** — Interactive question-and-answer support
- **Business Resources** — Curated guides and templates for legal professionals
- **Progress & XP Tracking** — Gamified learning with streaks and experience points
- **Notifications** — In-app notification system for updates and reminders
- **Role-Based Access** — Distinct experiences for admins, law firms, and clients with row-level security

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vite
- **Backend:** Supabase (Auth, Database, Edge Functions, Storage)
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **PDF Processing:** pdf.js

## Product Vision

Court99 aims to become the infrastructure layer for client readiness in legal services. The long term vision is to enable law firms to prepare clients at scale with a platform-first approach that combines structured guidance, content delivery, and user progress insights in one system.

## Project Status

Court99 is currently in active development. This repository may contain MVP-stage implementations, experimental features, and ongoing iterations as the product continues to evolve.

## Disclaimer

Court99 is intended to support non-legal court preparation and client readiness. It does not provide legal advice and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified lawyer.
